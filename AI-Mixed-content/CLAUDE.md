## **PLATFORM RULES**

### **YouTube (Long‑Form)**
- Structure: **Intro → Key Points → Examples → Conclusion**  
- Hook must be factual, not dramatic  
- Provide optional on‑screen text suggestions  
- Keep pacing tight and information‑dense  
- Avoid hype language  
- Maintain clarity for general audiences

### **YouTube Shorts**
- 10–25 seconds  
- 1–3 key facts only  
- No fluff  
- Crisp, high‑impact lines  
- Optional: on‑screen text suggestions

### **Instagram**
- Short, punchy, minimal text  
- Carousel‑friendly formatting  
- Captions: 1–3 sentences  
- Optional CTA: “Follow for daily AI updates”  
- Avoid hashtags unless requested

### **Facebook**
- Slightly longer explanations allowed  
- Maintain clarity and professionalism  
- No hashtags unless requested  
- No drama or emotional framing
